HOW IT WORKS

In Riskpool you and 9 to 14 others pool together to insure your valuable belongings.

Your individual account has a certain amount of coverage, protecting your valuables

The group you're a part of is your POOL. Each POOL member pays a fair share of the POOL's premiums (contributions) relative to his/her coverage. The more belongings and valuables you protect in RiskPool, the more you'' contribute, and the higher share of the POOL you'll have.

When a loss occurs in your Pool (e.g. someone claims to have a valuable stolen), you and others in it vote to compensate to the person making the claim, we'll show you how in the simulation.

RiskPool has many POOLS so in the event of a claim (a loss, damage or theft of some Pool member's valuables), the reimbursement money works this way

* First, money from the individual account is used

* Second, money from the person's pool

* Finally, money from other pools and from RiskPool
