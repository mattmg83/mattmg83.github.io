The characters

You get to pick who you want to be!

Bruce

Bruce has a job that lets him work remotely a lot, so the three most valuable things to him are his MacBook Air he carries everywhere, his fishing gear he takes to the river whenever he can, and his big flat screen TV.

Sue

Sue is a "glue" person, she know a lot of people and is friends with everyone. Not everybody knows what she does for a living, but her life is built around her bike, her surfboard for the rare times she's alone, and her homebrewing kit.

Ned

Ned is a creative friend, he is amateur photographer, rarely seen without his camera. He loves music, and bought himselves some turntables recently to have fun at home with is huge collection of vynils
