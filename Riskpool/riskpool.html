Choose your own adventure

This simulation is like a "choose your own adventure" book, you can pick the character you want to play and make a few choices along the way.

The main plot is that you're signing up for Riskpool, where you're grouped with 9 other people and you each put in a little bit of money to insure some of your belongings.

The simulation pretends to last a full year and shows you what happens if you or someone else in your pool suffers a loss or damage to some of their valuable belongings. 


The big idea is that unlike insurance, Riskpool is really a community and it has your back.

If you need help, your pool members are more likely to help than an actuarial software algorithm from BigInsuranceCo

If you don't need help you may even recover some of your investment and build wealth. Have you tried calling your insurer for money back? ...I didn't think so.... ;)
